Important Festivals of Tamilnadu



NEW YEAR

The New Year is an event that happens when a culture celebrates the end of one year and the beginning of the next year.

PONGAL

Pongal is a four-day-long harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu and this festival is celebrated as a thanksgiving ceremony for the years harvest.

DIWALI

Diwali brings a lot of happiness and delightful moments for everyone. They get to meet their family, friends and relatives and share greetings and gifts.

BAKRID

Muslims commemorate Bakrid to mark the Prophet Ibrahims willingness to sacrifice his own son as an act of obedience and devotion towards God.

SARASWATHI AND AYUTHA POOJA

Saraswathi puja, the Hindu deity of education. Ayudha puja, weapons and tools are worshipped

CHRISTMAS

Christmas is an annual festival commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ, a cultural celebration by billions of people around the world.

UZHAVAR THIRUNAL

Uzhavar Tirunal is Celebrated with a lot of happiness. The homes will be decorated with sugarcanes and turmeric.

RAMZAN

Ramadan is the most important month in the Islamic calendar. Ramadan is also known or called upon as Ramzan, Ramadhan, Ramathan.

KARTHIGAI DEEPAM

Karthigai is essentially a festival of lamps. It is believed to ward off evil forces and usher in prosperity and joy

GOOD FRIDAY

Good Friday is a Christian holiday commemorating the crucifixion of Jesus and his death at Calvary.
